## Mondavi Winery & Golden Hills Vineyard

### Overview
**Mondavi Winery** is the family's operation, centered around **Golden Hills Vineyard**, their primary vineyard in Napa Valley. The vineyard takes its name from the way the California sun paints the hills in shades of gold, creating a landscape of breathtaking beauty.

The vineyard spans twenty hectares, divided into distinct blocks, each with its own name, its own character, its own story. The soil is primarily volcanic, which gives the wine its distinctive minerality and structure. The perfect climate â€” warm days, cool nights, consistent sunshine â€” creates the ideal conditions for Cabernet Sauvignon, the grape that has made Napa Valley famous.

### The Named Blocks

#### Sunset Ridge
The block that catches the evening sun, where the Cabernet Sauvignon develops its most complex flavors. This is where the most age-worthy wines come from, where the perfect combination of sun and soil creates something special. Sarah's father always said that Sunset Ridge was where the vineyard showed its generosity, where nature gave its best.

#### Morning Glory
The block that faces east, catching the morning sun. The grapes here ripen early, developing fruit-forward flavors. This is where the most approachable wines come from, where the sun's warmth is most apparent.

#### The Original Block
The first block planted by Giuseppe Mondavi in 1943. The vines here are the oldest on the property, their roots reaching deep into the volcanic soil. This is where the Mondavi legacy began, where the family's commitment to excellence was first established. The wine from here is always special, always a reminder of where the family came from.

#### The Experiment
A block where Sarah tries new varietals and techniques. This is where innovation happens, where the future is being created, where the Mondavi legacy continues to evolve. Sarah says that The Experiment is where possibility becomes reality, where dreams become wine.
