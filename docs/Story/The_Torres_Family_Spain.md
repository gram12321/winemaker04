### Overview

### The Named Plots

#### El Campo Rojo (The Red Field)
The plot with the most intense red clay soil, where the Tempranillo grapes develop their boldest flavors. This is where the most powerful wines come from, where the land's intensity is most apparent. #### La Ladera del Sol (The Sunny Slope)
A plot that faces south, catching the sun from dawn to dusk. The grapes here ripen earliest, developing the most fruit-forward flavors. This is where the most approachable wines come from, where the sun's generosity is most apparent.

#### El ViÃ±edo Antiguo (The Old Vineyard)

#### La Terraza (The Terrace)
A plot that has been terraced into the hillside, its vines growing on narrow ledges. Bodega Torres follows the rhythm of the seasons, each one bringing its own tasks, its own challenges:
